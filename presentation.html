Our Vision:

Our vision is to establish Khalil Center Rockford as a comprehensive Islamic Family Wellness Center, while also supporting youth mentoring and offering career counseling services. By leveraging our partnership with Khalil Center's School of Islamic Psychology and utilizing their robust parent coaching curriculum, we aim to make Rockford a model for holistic parenting, youth development, and community well-being. Our programs, informed by community feedback and a thorough needs assessment, have already shown significant improvements in individual and family behaviors. With a data-driven approach, we are committed to providing tailored support to meet the diverse needs of our community. Your support and duas are deeply appreciated as we work toward building a resilient and thriving community.

Why Allocate
Zakat to Muslim
Mental Health?

A sacred obligation, a community need

  • Mental illness can render individuals unable to work, provide, or function — making them eligible Zakat recipients under the category of the poor and needy.
  • Many Muslims suffer in silence due to stigma, unable to access culturally competent care — your Zakat can bridge that gap.
  • Investing in mental health strengthens families, protects youth, and preserves the well-being of the entire Ummah.
  • The Prophet (SAW) said: "The believers in their mutual kindness, compassion, and sympathy are like one body." — Mental health is part of that body.
