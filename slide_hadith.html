The Three Thirds of Ramadan

1st Third
Days 1–10
❤️

Mercy

Rahma

Allah's mercy descends upon those who fast and seek His closeness.

2nd Third
Days 11–20
🙏

Forgiveness

Maghfira

A time to seek forgiveness and repent for past sins.

3rd Third
Days 21–30
🔥

Salvation

Najat min al-Nar

Salvation from the Hellfire for those who persevere in worship.

— Narrated by Salman al-Farisi (RA)
